SRIWIJAYA CIPTA KREASINDO - “SJ”

PT Sriwijaya Cipta Kreasindo was established in 2022. It operates in the field of branding production services in retail, focusing on design, production, and installation based in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Although our company is newly established, we have a team of experienced professionals with more than 10 years of experience, and we will continue to innovate following the developments in the branding industry.

We have successfully completed numerous projects in collaboration with national and multinational companies. With this experience, we are confident in our ability to grow and advance together at Sriwijaya Cipta Kreasindo.


OUR VISSION

By combining the semiotic approach of Design and Visual Communication, we present products that are not only creative and highly aesthetic but also help project a strategic position between intense market competition through special offerings and professional placement. This enables us to gain the full trust of customers and occupy a top position in the field of production and advertising.


OUR MISSION

Understanding the importance of service excellence as a performance benchmark to achieve continuity in improving quality in every production process, as well as realizing creative innovation through technological advancements.
